DOE Critical Infrastructures

Utilized Heroku, Cytoscape.js, Deck.gl, and python in order to create a simulation of a city and their infrastructures for Argonne National Laboratory. Worked with another intern where I focused on the web/python aspect while my partner worked on the unity aspect. Created a web scraper in order to get list of infrastructures aka list of water stations, power plants, emergency services from websites which was turned into JSON and CSV for my map rendition. The list had addresses of the buildings, which I used in a geolocating algorithm in order to get their latitude and longitudes. Features an embedded Unity 3D Simulation as well.

OnlyBikes
